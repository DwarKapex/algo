Insert 50000 numbers in range [0:49999]

Sorted insert: 15702 ms
Random insert: 85 ms

Search 5000 random numbers:
Sorted tree search: 1780 ms

Random tree search: 11 ms

Remove 5000 random numbers:
Sorted tree remove: 5701 ms

Random tree remove: 13 ms

Summary: inserting (searching and removing) sorted values, we get result similar to list by EOD and insertion takes O(N). Inserting random numbers is insertion with O(log(N)) time complexity
